How It Works

From evidence to report in 5 steps

No manual timeline building. No complex software. Just upload and get results.

01

Upload Evidence

Drop video, images, or audio files into the workstation. Supports MP4, JPG, PNG, MP3, and more.

02

AI Analysis

System detects people, vehicles, objects, and actions using multimodal AI. No manual tagging required.

03

Timeline Reconstruction

Events are automatically arranged into a chronological investigation timeline with timestamps.

04

Evidence Frame Extraction

Critical frames are captured and linked to timeline events for verification and reporting.

05

Generate Report

Export a structured forensic report (PDF/JSON) with executive summary, timeline, and evidence frames.

Key Features

Built for investigators, not engineers

Every feature is designed to save time and improve investigation quality.

⏱️

Timeline Reconstruction

AI converts raw footage into chronological event timelines automatically. No manual timestamping.

🖼️

Evidence Frame Linking

Each timeline event is tied to extracted video frames for verification and chain of custody.

📊

Investigation Reports

Privacy-First Processing

Person & Object Detection

Detect people, vehicles, weapons, bags, and other objects appearing in evidence.

🎬

Multimodal Analysis

Supports video, images, and audio investigation in a single workflow.
