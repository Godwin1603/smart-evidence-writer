The Problem

Evidence Analysis Shouldn’t Take Days.

Law enforcement officers spend 4–8 hours manually reviewing CCTV footage, documenting violations, identifying suspects, and drafting forensic reports. Critical evidence details get missed. Reports lack structure. Cases get delayed.

Alfa Hawk eliminates that bottleneck. Upload your evidence, and our AI delivers a structured, legally formatted report — complete with extracted frames, identified persons, vehicle plates, violations, risk assessments, and investigative recommendations.
