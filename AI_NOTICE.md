# AI Notice

This repository and related Alfa Hawk services integrate external AI systems to assist with evidence interpretation, report drafting, and event reconstruction.

## Third-Party AI Services

Alfa Hawk may use third-party AI and multimodal model providers for tasks such as:

- image and video interpretation
- event and scene summarization
- object and subject description
- timeline and report generation

Availability and behavior of these services may change over time.

## Important Limitations

AI-generated outputs may be incorrect, incomplete, inconsistent, biased, or contextually wrong. This includes but is not limited to:

- incorrect scene interpretations
- inaccurate subject or object descriptions
- missed events or hallucinated events
- flawed timelines
- misleading confidence signals

## Required Verification

This software integrates external AI services for evidence interpretation. AI-generated outputs may contain errors or misinterpretations. Users are responsible for validating all results.

All generated findings, observations, and reports must be independently reviewed and verified by qualified investigators or other appropriate professionals before being used in legal, regulatory, disciplinary, or operational decisions.

## No Substitute for Professional Review

Alfa Hawk is an assistive analysis tool, not a replacement for:

- certified forensic examination
- legal review
- evidentiary authentication
- expert investigative judgment

## No Warranty

AI-assisted features are provided without any guarantee of factual accuracy, evidentiary sufficiency, admissibility, or fitness for a particular purpose.
